Not-so-sure Assassin

« back to recipes

An assassin with a heart. The sole purpose was to move the bad guys to another plane. The last time I checked, she was investigating a leader of a shady organization. She can't wait to move the guy to the plane of nowhere.

Ingredients

  • A woman who loves needles and kicking balls
  • A bestseller novel that was written by a teenage girl, which was actually rewritten by a Math proffessor
  • And here the shady organization posed as a religion
  • Some small people
  • A second moon, a little smaller, hanging beside the original one

Directions

Highly reactive chemicals, wear suitable protective clothing and gloves.

Handle the ingredients with care. Carefully put them in a boiling pot one by one and in order. In case of contact with skin or eyes, rinse immediately with plenty of water and seek 1Q84 advice.
