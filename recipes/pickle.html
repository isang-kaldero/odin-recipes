Pickled Horse

« back to recipes

Not a horseradish but a pony for a short while, then turned into a horse. Actually from a novel Pickles and Ponies by Laura May

Ingredients

  • A heartless prince from the kingdom of Big Fish
  • A princess from Silverfish and Timbles that was an expert of the art of Le Poke
  • A princess from the kingdom of Fishlike Things who posed as a damsel in distress
  • A lot of ponies who spent half their lives doing acrobats on trees
  • A prince who cannot hear his heart and a horse who speaks for the said prince
  • Also, a very sensitive prince (not the same prince as above)
  • A handful of crazy stuff

Directions

Just shove 'em all in the pot, and that's that.
