Pickled Horse

Not a horseradish but a pony for a short while and turned into a horse. Actually from a novel Pickles and Ponies by Laura May

Ingredients

  • A heartless prince from Fishlike Things
  • A princess from Silverfish and Timbles that was an expert of the art of Le Poke
  • A princess who posed as a damsel in distress from the kingdom of Decorative Chimneys
  • A lot of ponies who spent have their lives doing acrobats on trees
  • Plentiful of crazy stuff until the end
